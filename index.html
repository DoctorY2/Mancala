Mancala


  • Fast Mancala (Ethiopian inspired).
  • 12 pits total, 4 stones in each pit.
  • You play the bottom row. Yonas (AI) plays the top row.
  • Stones are distributed one by one clockwise.
  • If the last stone lands on the opponent side and that pit has 2 or 3 stones, you capture them.
  • The game lasts 15 rounds (you + AI).
  • The player with the most captured stones wins.
