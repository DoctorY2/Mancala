Mancala


  • Fast Mancala inspired by Ethiopian Gebeta.
  • 12 pits total, 4 stones in each pit.
  • You play the bottom row. Yonas (AI) plays the top row.
  • Select one pit on your turn.
  • Stones move one by one clockwise.
  • If your last stone lands on the opponent side and that pit has 2 or 3 stones, you capture them.
  • The game lasts 9 rounds (you + AI).
  • The player with the most captured stones wins.
